Those working in the information sector have some of the most diverse and lucrative career opportunities in the entire job market. Ranging from sysadmins , who have one of the highest salaries outside of the medical field to web developers , who don't typically don't even need a college degree, the IT field has a place for anyone.

...In Any Field

Programming isn't just useful in the information sector. Building a website, automating Excel documents and sending emails along with everything else you can do programmatically are universal skills that will appeal to employers in any field.
